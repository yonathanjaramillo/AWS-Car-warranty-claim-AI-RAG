# Toyota Motor Corporation — Warranty Policy Guidelines 2024
## Section 1: Basic Warranty
- Duration: 3 years or 36,000 miles
- Covers defects in materials and workmanship
- All Toyota and Lexus vehicles included

## Section 2: Powertrain Warranty
- Duration: 5 years or 60,000 miles
- Engine, transmission, front-wheel drive, rear-wheel drive components

## Section 3: Hybrid System Warranty
- Duration: 8 years or 100,000 miles
- Hybrid battery, hybrid control module, hybrid inverter

## Section 4: Approved Labor Operations
- 12345: Engine oil and filter — basic warranty coverage
- 12346: Engine tune up — covered if failure due to defect
- 23456: Transmission service — powertrain warranty
- 34567: Hybrid battery inspection — hybrid warranty
- Approved labor rate: $120/hour

## Section 5: Parts Requirements
- Genuine Toyota parts required for all warranty repairs
- No aftermarket substitutions permitted
- Parts must be ordered through Toyota dealer network

## Section 6: Claim Requirements
- VIN verification mandatory
- Digital photos required for claims over $1,000
- Pre-authorization required for claims over $3,000
- Maximum claim without pre-auth: $3,000

## Section 7: Exclusions
- Wear items: brake pads, tires, filters, belts
- Accident damage
- Flood or environmental damage
- Non-Toyota parts or modifications
