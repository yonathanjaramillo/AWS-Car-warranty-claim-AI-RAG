# General Motors — Warranty Policy Guidelines 2024
## Section 1: Coverage Overview
General Motors provides comprehensive warranty coverage for Chevrolet, GMC, Buick, and Cadillac vehicles sold through authorized GM dealers.

## Section 2: Limited Warranty Coverage
- Duration: 3 years or 36,000 miles, whichever occurs first
- Covers defects in material or workmanship
- Includes 24-hour roadside assistance

## Section 3: Powertrain Limited Warranty
- Duration: 5 years or 60,000 miles
- Covers: Engine, transmission, transfer case, drive axle
- Labor op codes: A10001, A10002, B20001, B20002

## Section 4: Approved Labor Operations
### Section 4.1: Engine Operations
- A10001: Engine oil and filter service — approved, basic warranty
- A10002: Engine diagnostic — approved, basic warranty
- A20001: Engine replacement — approved, powertrain warranty
- Approved labor rate: $130/hour maximum

### Section 4.2: Transmission Operations
- B20001: Transmission service — approved, powertrain warranty
- B20002: Transmission replacement — approved, powertrain warranty
- All transmission parts must be AC Delco or GM genuine parts

## Section 5: Claim Submission Requirements
- Claims must be submitted within 90 days of repair
- Maximum claim: $4,500 without pre-authorization
- Parts markup maximum: 35% over dealer cost

## Section 6: Coverage Exclusions
- Normal maintenance items
- Accident or collision damage
- Environmental damage
- Unauthorized modifications
